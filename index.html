Ask a Question →
What is Panchakarma and how long does the program take? +
Panchakarma is Ayurveda's classical five-fold detoxification and rejuvenation procedure. It consists of preparatory procedures (Poorvakarma), main procedures (Pradhanakarma), and post-procedures (Paschatkarma). A proper Panchakarma program typically takes 14 to 28 days, depending on your health condition and the doctor's prescription. Shorter programs of 7 days focus on preparatory and partial Panchakarma.
How do I arrange my travel and visa for treatment? +
We provide a formal medical invitation letter from our hospital, which you can use to apply for an Indian Medical Visa. Our international patient coordinator will guide you through the process and help with travel logistics. We recommend applying at least 4–6 weeks before your intended travel date. Coimbatore International Airport (CJB) is the nearest airport, approximately 50 km from our hospital.
What conditions do you treat at Eden Ayurveda? +
We treat a wide range of conditions including: arthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, spondylitis, disc prolapse, sciatica, neurological disorders, Parkinson's disease, psoriasis, eczema, digestive disorders (IBS, Crohn's), PCOD, infertility, thyroid disorders, obesity, diabetes management, migraine, insomnia, stress and anxiety, and more. We also offer wellness and rejuvenation programs for healthy individuals.
Is Ayurvedic treatment safe for everyone? +
Classical Ayurvedic treatments, when administered by qualified physicians after proper diagnosis, are generally safe for most individuals. Our doctors conduct a thorough assessment before prescribing any treatment to ensure it is appropriate for your specific health condition, age, and constitution (Prakriti). Pregnant women, individuals on complex medication regimens, or those with serious medical conditions will be assessed on a case-by-case basis.
What should I bring for my stay at Eden Ayurveda? +
We recommend bringing loose, comfortable clothing (old clothes that may get oil stains), any recent medical reports or investigation results, your regular medications (our doctors will advise if any changes are needed), a sun hat and light layers, and an open, patient mindset. We provide traditional Ayurvedic garments for treatments. All herbal medicines, oils, and treatment materials are provided by us.
Can I continue my regular medications during Ayurvedic treatment? +
This depends on your specific medications and conditions. Our physicians will review your current medications during the initial consultation. For most maintenance medications (blood pressure, thyroid, diabetes), we do not advise abrupt stopping. Allopathic and Ayurvedic treatments can often be integrated safely. Any changes to your regular medications will only be made gradually and with your full awareness and consent.
How do I book and what is the payment process? +
You can initiate your booking by filling the inquiry form on this website, emailing us, or contacting us via WhatsApp. After your initial online consultation, we will provide a detailed treatment and cost proposal. International payments can be made via bank transfer, PayPal, or credit/debit card. We require a 30% advance to confirm your booking, with the balance payable on arrival.
